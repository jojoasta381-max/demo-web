What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive engineering expertise across six core disciplines, tailored precisely to your project requirements.

01

BIM & VDC

Building Information Modeling and Virtual Design & Construction for data centres, district cooling plants, and commercial buildings. Full project coordination using Revit and industry-standard platforms.

02

Structural Engineering

Precision structural analysis, design, and assessment for complex projects. We ensure structural integrity and compliance while delivering innovative solutions that optimize material efficiency.

03

Telecom Infrastructure

Complete telecommunication tower design and analysis including mount analysis, structural assessment, and material specification for new installations and upgrades across India.

04

NDT Testing

Non-Destructive Testing and analysis for government and commercial infrastructure. We verify structural integrity using advanced NDT methods without compromising the asset's lifespan.

05

CAD & Machine Design

Advanced CAD engineering, machine component design, analysis, and fabrication for industrial and automotive sectors. Precision engineering from concept through prototyping to full production.

06

Business Development

Strategic business development and consultancy to strengthen company branding, personal branding, and market positioning for established engineering and construction firms.
